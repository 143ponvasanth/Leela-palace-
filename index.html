EXPERIENCE INDIA WITH THE LEELA

The Leela Palace Chennai

Evoking the spirit of Chettinad Palaces and their architectural splendour, The Leela Palace Chennai, a luxury hotel in Chennai, embodies a tapestry of historical influences blended with all modern luxuries. Nestled in 4.8 acres against the backdrop of the Bay of Bengal, this resplendent sea-facing palace, a 5-star hotel in Chennai, seamlessly blends the contemporary with the traditional.

Each turn reveals a visual feast: polished marble corridors, high, chandelier-lit ceilings, a remarkable collection of art and artefacts, and manicured gardens. Tuck into scrumptious global fare at our fine dining restaurants. As dusk falls, join us for the mesmerising ritual of lighting theepantham lamps to reverberating kombu blow-horns. Lose yourself in a soul-stirring Carnatic concert. Your suite, a haven of decadence, offers more than comfort - it is a window to Chennai's soul.

Jamavar

Savour the finest dishes from royal kitchens at our signature restaurant: from Raan-e-Jamavaar (spice-marinated, tandoor-grilled leg of lamb served with a splash of rum) to Dal Jamavar (black lentils simmered overnight and topped with cream).

Experiences

Art

Take a guided tour of the hotel and the 2000-year-old neighbourhood of Mylapore dotted with temples, eateries, and traditional homes to discover the myth and majesty of Tamizh culture.

Ceremonial Rituals

Play witness to the daily evening ritual where theepantham lamps are lit as the sonorous calls of kombu blow-horns fill the air. Follow it up with a relaxed musical sabha of classical South Indian instruments.

Culinary

Sample regional Tamizh and international delicacies at our in-house restaurants. Engage in a friendly cook-off with our expert chefs or learn The Leela’s secret cocktail recipes from our skilled mixologists.

Events

Weddings

Plan the wedding of your dreams at The Leela Palace Chennai’s enchanting venue by the shores of the Bay of Bengal. The sprawling gardens and spacious rooms are perfect for intimate gatherings and pre-wedding functions while the pristine beachfront offers a charming setting for celebrations and photography. For a grand reception or cocktail dinner, our spectacular ballroom awaits.

Celebrations

Transform your celebrations into timeless memories at The Leela Palace Chennai’s grand indoor spaces, with thematic decor and impeccable hospitality. Be it a milestone birthday, a graduation party or a graceful farewell, our versatile venues can cater to every kind of function.

Meetings

When people come together and ideas flow, business events transform from mundane to magical. Be it strategic huddles or multi-day conferences, turn your events into runaway successes at The Leela Palace Chennai’s breathtaking settings, backed by state-of-the-art tech and media facilities.
